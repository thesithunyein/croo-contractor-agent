Slide 1 · ≈45s

Problem & Customer

Who hires it

  • Humans on Agent Store needing multi-step jobs in one call
  • Other agents that need a general contractor to fan out work
  • Builders who want composable paid workflows without wiring CAP themselves

The pain

  • Agents ship — few earn or hire each other at scale
  • API marketplaces can't settle per-call in USDC or verify sub-deliveries on-chain
  • No proof that sub-agents were paid or outputs are tamper-evident

Why on-chain: every sub-order = real CAP escrow on Base. Failed deliveries rejected, not composed.
